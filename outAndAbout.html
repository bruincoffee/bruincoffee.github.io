The following dedication is at the base of this Paul Bunyan Statue in the Kenton district of Northwest Portland, Oregon.
"SUSCEEDED" instead of SUCCEEDED
A closer look.
"Romane" instead of Romaine
"SPRED" instead of SPRED
"THORTON WILDER" instead of THORNTON WILDER
"YOUR" instead of YOU'RE
"all are wonderful customers" instead of all OUR wonderful customers — and another incorrect "are" later in the note
Maybe not a mistake, but look close — one of these hands is flipping the bird!
A bird has definitely been flipped here in NW PDX!
"Weef of" instead of week of; also "marina" instead of marinara
"CALM SHELL" instead of CLAM SHELL; "BURSELLS SPROUTS" instead of BRUSSELS SPROUTS; "TUMERIC" instead of TURMERIC
"Itenerary" instead of Itinerary
